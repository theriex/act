Reflecting workflow through zero-cost tech:
Not My Normal is an informational resource for resisting normalization of hatred, oppression, and discrimination.

Not My Normal uses a Google calendar and a Membic theme to inform visitors of upcoming events and helpful resources. Site content is updated automatically when organization members post resource links or add calendar events. Website maintenance is essentially zero.
